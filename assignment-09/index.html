Instructable Sandwiches:

Ingredients:

  • Two slices of your favorite bread.
  • One table-spoon of your favorite jelly
  • Two table-spoons of your favorite peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Gather together the two slices of your favorite bread.
  2. Line one slice of bread with one table-spoon of your favorite jelly
  3. Line the remaining slice of bread with two table-spoons of your favorite peanut butter
  4. Press the two covered sides of the slices of bread together to form a sandwich
  5. Enjoy your peanut butter and jelly sandwich
