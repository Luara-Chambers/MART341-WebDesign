Egyptain Milk Chocolate Pumpkin Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice or 1 tablesppon apple pie spice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6 ounces milk chocolate chips (semi-sweet is ok)
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin)
  • 3/4 cup melted butter or 3/4 cup margarine
  • 6 eggs (slightly beaten) or 6 egg substitute

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Spray a tube pan with a nonstick spray.
  3. Set aside 1/2 cup of flour in a small bowl.
  4. Combine the pumpkin, melted butter, and eggs into a large bowl.
  5. Add the pumpkin mixture to the flour mixture and stir until combined. Then add the chocolate chip mixture. Stir until combined, then pour the batter into the tube pan.
  6. Bake for 60 minutes, test with wooden toothepick near center of cake to make sure it is cooked.
  7. Cool pan on wire rack for 15 minutes. Gently remove the cake from the pan and cool for 30 minutes. Cover with powdered sugar, or icing.
